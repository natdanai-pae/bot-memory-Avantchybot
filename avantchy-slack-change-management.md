# Slack Adoption And Change Management

User asked how to shift a team from LINE to Slack for office communication and management.

## Context

- Team has Gen Z staff and siloed working patterns.
- Goal is better communication, office coordination, and work management.

## Memory

When helping with Slack adoption, include:

- Why change: searchable work memory, channels by project/function, transparency.
- Change plan: pilot group, champions, simple channel taxonomy, rules of response.
- Training: short guides, examples, office hours.
- Governance: what stays in LINE vs Slack, escalation rules.
- Metrics: adoption, response time, task closure, cross-team collaboration.
